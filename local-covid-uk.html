Local data on the COVID-19 pandemic in the United Kingdom

To be able to track the development of the pandemic in the UK we built this page in which we are making the local UK data available in six charts. Updated daily.
by Hannah Ritchie, Edouard Mathieu and Max Roser
January 07, 2021

To be able to track the development of the pandemic in the United Kingdom, we are making the UK government’s data available in the following charts. We are updating the data daily.

→ For data on the rest of the world, see our COVID-19 Data Explorer.
